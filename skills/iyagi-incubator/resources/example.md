 Topic: first love
 Chosen level: 2A
 Target grammar count: 6

 Korean Story

 민수는 고등학교 때 첫사랑이 있었어요.
 그 사람은 같은 반 친구 지민이에요. 지민은 조용하지만 웃는 얼굴이 아주 예뻤어요.

 민수는 매일 학교에서 지민을 보고 싶었어요. 그래서 아침에 일찍 학교에 갔어요. 어느 날, 지민이 교실에서 책을 읽고 있었어요. 민수는 지민한테 “이 책 재미있어요?”라고 물었어요. 지
 민은 웃고 “네, 정말 좋아요. 다음에 같이 도서관에 갈래요?”라고 말했어요.

 민수는 너무 기뻤지만 말을 잘할 수 없었어요. 그래도 작은 목소리로 “네, 갈게요”라고 대답했어요. 그날부터 민수는 학교에 가는 것이 더 즐거웠어요.

 English Translation

 Minsu had a first love in high school.
 That person was Jimin, a friend in the same class. Jimin was quiet, but her smiling face was very pretty.

 Minsu wanted to see Jimin at school every day. So he went to school early in the morning. One day, Jimin was reading a book in the classroom. Minsu asked Jimin, “Is this book
 interesting?” Jimin smiled and said, “Yes, it’s really good. Do you want to go to the library together next time?”

 Minsu was very happy, but he couldn’t speak well. Still, in a small voice, he answered, “Yes, I’ll go.” From that day on, going to school became more enjoyable for Minsu.
